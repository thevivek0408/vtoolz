Crop Image — Frequently Asked Questions

How do I crop an image online?

First, upload your image by dragging it onto the page. Then drag the crop handles to select the area you want to keep. Finally, click "Crop & Download" and the cropped image saves to your device instantly.

Do my images get uploaded?

No. The cropping tool runs entirely in your browser using Cropper.js. As a result, your image never leaves your device. In contrast, online photo editors usually upload files to cloud servers.

Can I rotate images too?

Yes. In addition to cropping, you can rotate the image 90° at a time or flip it horizontally. Furthermore, you can reset all changes and start over at any time.

Can I crop to a specific aspect ratio?

Yes. The crop area is freely resizable. For example, drag it to a square shape for Instagram profile pictures or a 16:9 rectangle for video thumbnails.

Is this tool free?

Yes. Image cropping with Vibox is 100% free with no watermarks, no limits, and no account required.
