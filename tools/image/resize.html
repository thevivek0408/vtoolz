Resize Image — Frequently Asked Questions

How do I resize an image online?

First, upload your image by dragging it onto the page or clicking to browse. Then choose whether to resize by pixel dimensions or by target file size. Finally, click Resize and download the result instantly. The entire process runs in your browser.

Do my images get uploaded?

No. In contrast to cloud-based resizers, Vibox processes everything locally inside your browser. As a result, your photos and screenshots never leave your device.

Can I resize to a specific file size?

Yes. Switch to "By File Size" mode and enter a target in KB or MB. For example, enter 300 KB to prepare an image for an email attachment limit. The tool estimates the best quality and resolution to meet your target.

Does resizing maintain aspect ratio?

Yes. If you enter only a width, the height is calculated automatically to keep the original proportions. Similarly, entering only a height auto-calculates the width.

Can I resize multiple images at once?

Yes. Drop multiple images at once and all will be resized to the same dimensions. Furthermore, each result shows a preview with the new file size for easy comparison.

Is there a size limit?

There is no server limit. However, the practical limit depends on your device's available memory. Most devices handle images up to 50 MB without issues.
