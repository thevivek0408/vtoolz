Image Tools

All Image Tools — Free & Private

Vibox provides a complete set of image tools that run entirely in your browser. In contrast to services like TinyPNG or Canva, your images are never uploaded to any server. As a result, every photo, screenshot, and graphic you edit stays completely private on your device.

Compress & Optimize

  • Image Compressor — Reduce JPG, PNG, and WebP file sizes by up to 80% without visible quality loss. Furthermore, you can adjust the compression level from 10% to 100% with a live slider.

Resize & Transform

  • Resize Image — Change width and height by pixels or target a specific file size in KB/MB. Moreover, you can resize while maintaining the original aspect ratio.
  • Crop Image — Trim images to any dimension. For instance, crop a photo to a square for social media or a 16:9 ratio for presentations.

Convert

  • Image Converter — Convert between JPG, PNG, and WebP formats instantly. For example, convert a PNG screenshot to WebP for 40% smaller file sizes.

Edit & Create

  • Image Editor — Basic photo editing including brightness, contrast, and saturation adjustments.
  • Image Filters — Apply visual effects like grayscale, sepia, blur, and sharpening to your images.
  • Meme Generator — Create memes with custom text in seconds. In addition, the result downloads as a ready-to-share image.
  • Color Extractor — Extract the dominant colour palette from any uploaded image.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are all image tools free?

Yes. Every image tool on Vibox is 100% free with no watermarks, no limits, and no sign-up required. Furthermore, you can use each tool as many times as you need at no cost.

Do my images get uploaded?

No. All processing happens in your browser. As a result, your images never leave your device. In contrast, TinyPNG and similar services upload every image to their cloud servers for processing.

What formats are supported?

Most tools support JPG, PNG, and WebP. Additionally, some tools accept GIF, BMP, and SVG depending on your browser's native image support.

Can I process multiple images at once?

Yes. The compressor, resizer, and converter all support batch processing. For example, drop 20 photos at once and they will each be processed individually with a preview for every result.

Do the tools work offline?

Yes. Vibox is a Progressive Web App. After your first visit, each tool is cached. Subsequently, it loads and works fully without an internet connection.
