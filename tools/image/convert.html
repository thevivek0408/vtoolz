Image Converter — Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats can I convert?

The Vibox image converter supports conversion between JPG, PNG, and WebP. For example, you can convert a PNG screenshot to WebP for smaller file sizes, or convert WebP to JPG for universal compatibility.

Do my images get uploaded?

No. Conversion happens entirely in your browser using the Canvas API. As a result, your images never leave your device. In contrast, online converters like CloudConvert or Zamzar upload every file to their servers.

Should I convert to WebP?

WebP typically produces files 25–40% smaller than JPG or PNG with similar quality. However, some older apps and email clients may not support WebP. Therefore, choose JPG if maximum compatibility is important, or WebP if smaller size matters most.

Can I convert multiple images at once?

Yes. Drop several images at once and they will all be converted to your chosen format. Furthermore, each converted file can be downloaded individually.

What is the difference between JPG and PNG?

JPG uses lossy compression, which means smaller files but some quality loss. In contrast, PNG uses lossless compression and supports transparency. As a result, PNG is ideal for logos and graphics with text, while JPG is better for photographs.
