Image to PDF — Frequently Asked Questions

How do I combine images into a PDF?

First, drag and drop your images onto the page or click to browse. Then reorder them if needed. Finally, click "Convert to PDF" and the combined file downloads instantly. Each image becomes one page.

What image formats are supported?

The tool accepts JPG, PNG, and WebP images. Furthermore, you can mix different formats in the same PDF.

Do my images get uploaded?

No. Conversion happens entirely in your browser using pdf-lib. As a result, your photos and scans are never sent to any server. In contrast, most online converters upload every file.

Can I reorder pages before creating the PDF?

Yes. After uploading, drag the thumbnails to rearrange the order. The final PDF follows whatever sequence you set.
