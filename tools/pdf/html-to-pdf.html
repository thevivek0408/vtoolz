HTML to PDF — Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert HTML to PDF?

First, paste or type your HTML markup into the editor. Then click "Convert to PDF" and the rendered output downloads instantly. The entire conversion runs locally in your browser using html2pdf.js.

Does it support CSS styles?

Yes. Inline styles and common CSS properties are rendered in the output PDF. However, external stylesheets are not loaded, so include styles inline for best results.

Do my files get uploaded?

No. In contrast to cloud converters, Vibox processes the HTML entirely in your browser. As a result, your code and content remain private.

Can I convert a full webpage?

This tool accepts raw HTML. For example, you can copy the source of a page and paste it here. Nevertheless, complex layouts with external images may not render identically.

Is this tool free?

Yes. HTML to PDF conversion with Vibox is 100% free — no account, no limits, and no watermarks.
