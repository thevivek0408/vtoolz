Merge PDF — Frequently Asked Questions

How do I merge PDF files?

Drag and drop your PDF files onto the upload area, or click to browse and select them. Once uploaded, the files appear in a list. Drag to reorder them if needed, then click the Merge PDFs button. Your combined PDF downloads automatically — no sign-in required.

Do my PDFs get uploaded to a server?

No. Vibox uses PDF-lib, an open-source PDF library compiled to run inside your browser using WebAssembly. Your PDF files are processed entirely in your device's memory and never transmitted to any server. This is fundamentally different from iLovePDF, SmallPDF, or Adobe's online tools, which all upload your files to remote servers.

Is there a limit on how many PDFs I can merge?

There is no hard limit. You can merge as many PDFs as your browser's available memory allows. Most modern devices can comfortably handle 20–50 PDFs totalling hundreds of megabytes in one session. Since there is no server, there is no upload quota or rate limit.

Can I reorder the PDFs before merging?

Yes. After uploading, all files appear in a drag-and-drop list. Drag any file to change its position. The final merged PDF follows the order shown in the list. You can also remove individual files from the list if you change your mind before merging.

Is merging PDF files free?

Yes. Merging PDFs with Vibox is completely free with no usage limits, no watermarks on the output PDF, and no account required. You can merge as many PDFs as many times as you like at no cost.

Can I merge PDFs on my phone?

Yes. Vibox works on mobile browsers including Chrome for Android and Safari for iOS. Select PDFs from your Files app or Google Drive on mobile and merge them directly in your phone's browser. No app download is needed.

Does the merged PDF preserve the content of each page?

Yes. PDF-lib copies each page from the source PDFs at full fidelity. All text, images, vector graphics, and page layout are preserved exactly as in the original documents. The output is a standard PDF file that opens in any PDF viewer.

What is the maximum file size for merging?

There is no server-imposed size limit. The practical maximum depends on your device's available RAM. Most laptops and phones can merge PDFs totalling up to 200 MB without issues. Very large scanned PDF documents may be slower to process due to their size.
