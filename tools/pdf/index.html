PDF Tools

Every PDF Tool You Need — Free & Private

Vibox offers a complete suite of free online PDF tools that run entirely in your browser. In contrast to cloud services like iLovePDF or SmallPDF, your files are never uploaded to any server. As a result, even confidential contracts, tax returns, and medical documents stay 100% private.

Organize & Combine

  • Merge PDF — Combine multiple PDFs into a single document. Furthermore, you can drag to reorder files before merging.
  • Split PDF — Extract specific pages from any PDF. For example, pull out pages 3, 7, and 12 into a new file.
  • Reorder PDF — Rearrange page order with a visual drag-and-drop interface.
  • Delete PDF Pages — Remove unwanted pages from a PDF without re-uploading.
  • Duplicate PDF Pages — Copy pages within a PDF for forms or handouts.
  • Reverse PDF — Flip the entire page order of a PDF document.
  • Extract PDF Pages — Pick and save only the pages you need.

Compress & Optimize

  • Compress PDF — Reduce PDF file size by up to 80%. Choose Low, Medium, or High compression presets. Additionally, text and vector graphics stay sharp at every setting.
  • Flatten PDF — Flatten interactive form fields into static content for sharing or printing.

Convert

  • PDF to Word — Convert PDF to editable DOCX format. However, scanned PDFs produce limited text without OCR.
  • PDF to Image — Export every page as a high-quality JPG or PNG image. Moreover, you can download all pages as a ZIP.
  • Image to PDF — Convert JPG, PNG, or WebP images into a single PDF.
  • Word to PDF — Convert DOCX documents to standard PDF files.
  • Excel to PDF — Convert spreadsheets to PDF for easy sharing.
  • HTML to PDF — Save any HTML markup or webpage as a clean PDF.

Edit & Protect

  • Rotate PDF — Rotate pages 90°, 180°, or 270°. For instance, fix upside-down scans in seconds.
  • Watermark PDF — Add custom text watermarks with full control over font, size, colour, opacity, and position.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are all PDF tools free?

Yes. Every PDF tool on Vibox is completely free with no usage limits, no watermarks, and no account required. Furthermore, there are no file size restrictions or daily caps.

Do my PDFs get uploaded?

No. All processing runs locally in your browser using PDF-lib and PDF.js. As a result, your documents never leave your device. In contrast, services like SmallPDF and iLovePDF upload every file to their servers.

Can I use these tools on my phone?

Yes. All tools work on mobile browsers including Chrome for Android and Safari for iOS. In addition, you can install Vibox as a PWA for a native app experience on your phone.

Do the tools work offline?

Yes. Vibox is a Progressive Web App. After your first visit, tools are cached automatically. Subsequently, they load and work fully even without an internet connection.
