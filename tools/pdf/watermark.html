PDF Watermark — Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a watermark to a PDF?

First, upload your PDF by dragging it onto the page. Then type your watermark text and customize the font, size, color, and position. Finally, click "Apply" and download the watermarked PDF. The entire process runs locally in your browser.

Can I customize the watermark appearance?

Yes. You can choose the text content, font family, font size, color with opacity, and placement on the page. As a result, you can create subtle "DRAFT" stamps or bold "CONFIDENTIAL" overlays.

Does the watermark appear on all pages?

Yes. The watermark is applied to every page of the PDF. However, you can adjust the opacity so it remains visible without obscuring the content underneath.

Do my PDFs get uploaded?

No. In contrast to online watermark services, Vibox processes everything locally. As a result, sensitive documents remain on your device at all times.
