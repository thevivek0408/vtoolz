PDF to Image — Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert a PDF to images?

First, upload your PDF by dragging it onto the page. Then each page renders as a high-quality JPG preview. Finally, download individual pages or click "Download All as ZIP" to save them all at once.

What image format are the output files?

Pages are exported as JPG images. JPG was chosen because it offers the best balance of quality and file size. As a result, the images are easy to share via email or messaging apps.

Do my PDFs get uploaded?

No. In contrast to cloud-based converters, Vibox renders each page locally using pdf.js and the Canvas API. As a result, your documents stay completely private.

Can I convert a multi-page PDF?

Yes. Every page in the PDF is converted automatically. Furthermore, you can download all pages as a single ZIP file for convenience.

Is this tool free?

Yes. PDF to image conversion with Vibox is 100% free — no watermarks, no limits, and no account required.
