PDF to Word — Frequently Asked Questions

How does PDF to Word conversion work?

Vibox uses PDF.js to parse your PDF and extract all text content on each page. This text is then structured into a Word-compatible DOCX file using the docx.js library running entirely in your browser. No uploads, no server, no API calls — the whole conversion happens locally on your device.

Do my PDF files get uploaded to a server?

No. Unlike Adobe's online PDF to Word converter, iLovePDF, or SmallPDF — which all process your files on remote servers — Vibox converts your PDF to Word entirely inside your browser. Your document never leaves your device, making it safe for confidential, legal, or personal files.

Is the converted Word document editable?

Yes. The output is a standard .docx file that opens in Microsoft Word, Google Docs, LibreOffice, and any other Word-compatible application. The text is fully editable — you can modify, reformat, copy, and save the document like any other Word file.

What types of PDFs convert best?

PDFs originally created digitally from Word, Excel, or other programs convert best because they contain actual embedded text data. Scanned PDFs (images of pages) cannot have text extracted without OCR. The current tool does not include an OCR engine, so scanned PDFs will produce minimal text output.

Will the formatting and layout be preserved?

Basic text structure — paragraphs and line breaks — is preserved. Complex layouts such as multi-column text, tables, headers, footers, and images are not reproduced in the DOCX output. The focus is on extracting clean, readable text content. For pixel-perfect layout conversion, a desktop application may give better results.

Is PDF to Word conversion free?

Yes, completely free. No account, no subscription, no watermark on the output. Unlike services that cap free conversions per day, Vibox imposes no usage limits.

What is the maximum PDF size I can convert?

There is no server-side limit. The practical limit depends on your browser's available memory. PDFs with hundreds of pages of text convert quickly on most modern devices. Very large PDFs with complex graphics may take a few extra seconds.

Can I convert Word back to PDF?

Yes. Vibox also includes a Word to PDF converter that converts DOCX files to PDF entirely in the browser. You can also use the HTML to PDF tool to save any web page as a PDF document.
