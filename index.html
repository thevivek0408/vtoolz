Why Choose Vibox?

Vibox is a growing collection of 90+ free online tools designed for everyone who values speed and privacy. In contrast to traditional cloud services, every single tool on Vibox runs entirely inside your browser. As a result, your files never leave your device — no uploads to remote servers, no data collection, no sign-up required, and zero tracking.

For example, you can merge PDF files, compress images for your website, convert PDF to Word, resize photos, split PDFs, generate QR codes, format JSON, record your screen, or calculate your BMI — all in one place. Furthermore, there are no subscriptions, no watermarks, and no file size limits.

Moreover, Vibox works offline. Once loaded, each tool is cached by a service worker so you can use it on a plane or in areas with poor connectivity. In addition, Vibox is installable as a PWA on Chrome, Edge, and Safari for a full native app experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are Vibox tools completely free?

Yes. Every tool on Vibox is 100% free with no hidden fees, subscriptions, or paywalls. Moreover, there are no usage limits, no watermarks on outputs, and no account required. In other words, you can use every tool as many times as you need, with files of any size, at no cost whatsoever.

Do my files get uploaded to a server?

No — and this is the most important difference between Vibox and services like SmallPDF, iLovePDF, or TinyPNG. Instead, all processing happens directly in your browser using JavaScript and WebAssembly. As a result, your files never leave your device. Therefore, your confidential documents, private images, and sensitive data stay completely under your control.

How does Vibox compress images without uploading them?

The Image Compressor uses the HTML5 Canvas API to read your image locally and re-encode it at your chosen quality level. Specifically, the entire process runs in your browser's memory. Furthermore, no network request is made at any point during compression. As a result, the smaller image is offered as an instant browser download without ever touching a server.

How does Vibox merge PDF files without a server?

The PDF Merge tool uses PDF-lib, a powerful open-source library compiled to run inside your browser. First, PDF-lib reads each uploaded PDF entirely in memory. Then it combines the pages in your chosen order and, finally, produces a new PDF for you to download — all without any server connection.

Does Vibox work offline?

Yes. Vibox is a Progressive Web App (PWA). After you first visit a tool, a service worker automatically caches it on your device. Subsequently, on your next visit — even without internet — the tool loads and works fully offline. For example, this is especially useful for compressing images or working with PDFs while travelling.

Can I install Vibox as an app on my phone or desktop?

Yes. Vibox is installable as a PWA on Chrome, Edge, and Safari. For instance, on desktop, look for the install icon in the address bar. Similarly, on mobile, use "Add to Home Screen". Once installed, Vibox opens like a native app with full offline support and no browser chrome.

Is there a file size limit for PDF or image tools?

There is no server-imposed size limit because all processing happens locally in your browser. However, the practical limit is your device's available RAM. In general, most modern devices handle PDFs up to 100 MB and images up to 50 MB comfortably. Nevertheless, very large files may be slower depending on your hardware.

What browsers does Vibox support?

Vibox works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari, and Opera. Most tools require ES2020 support and the File API. For best performance, we recommend Chrome or Edge. Furthermore, mobile browsers on Android and iOS are supported for the vast majority of tools.

What image formats does the Image Compressor support?

The Vibox Image Compressor supports JPG, PNG, and WebP. Additionally, you adjust the quality level from 10% to 100% and see the compressed file size in real time before downloading. For instance, at the default 80% setting, quality loss is virtually invisible for most photos and graphics.

How is Vibox different from SmallPDF, iLovePDF, or TinyPNG?

Privacy and cost are the two biggest differences. Specifically, SmallPDF, iLovePDF, and TinyPNG all upload your files to remote servers for processing. In contrast, Vibox never uploads anything — all processing is entirely local in your browser. Furthermore, Vibox is completely free with no file limits, covers far more tool categories (PDF, Image, Text, Developer, Media, QR, Math, Time, Games), and works offline without an internet connection.

Overall, Vibox gives you everything you need — for free, privately, and without limits. So go ahead and browse all 90+ free tools, or use the search bar at the top of this page to find exactly what you need.
